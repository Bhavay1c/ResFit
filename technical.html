Technical Overview of ResiFit

ResiFit is built with a combination of advanced hardware and software components designed to deliver a seamless workout experience. Here’s a breakdown of the technical components:

  • ESP32 Microcontroller: Handles wireless communication with the mobile app via Bluetooth and controls the motor system to adjust the resistance in real-time.
  • Motor-Driven Resistance Bands: The resistance is provided by coiled strings that loop through the arms and adjust tension based on the motor’s control, which is customizable via the app.
  • Bluetooth Connectivity: The device connects to your smartphone through Bluetooth, providing real-time feedback and enabling resistance customization.
  • Mobile App: The companion app is designed to provide a user-friendly interface, allowing users to adjust workout intensity, track progress, and monitor feedback in real-time.
  • Power Supply: The device is powered by a rechargeable battery, providing hours of continuous use with energy-efficient motor controls.

How Does ResiFit Work?

ResiFit integrates resistance bands controlled via a motor connected to the ESP32. The bands wrap around your arms, offering adjustable resistance as you move. The app communicates with the microcontroller to set the desired resistance level, ensuring a customized and effective workout experience.
