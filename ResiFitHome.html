ResiFit is an innovative wearable gym equipment designed to offer adjustable resistance for arm workouts using coiled strings controlled by a motor. The resistance levels are managed through a phone app connected via Bluetooth to an ESP32 microcontroller, allowing users to customize their workout intensity on the go.

Solution -- Introducing ResiFit

ResiFit is an innovative wearable fitness device designed to enhance strength training while honing athletic skills. The device features a vest which user wears which have a main component on the back of it with adjustable resistance bands controlled by a motor, allowing users to customize the weight based on their fitness level. Using Bluetooth connectivity, the companion app provides real-time feedback and performance tracking, enabling users to monitor progress and adjust their workouts effectively. The ergonomic design ensures a comfortable fit, allowing for a full range of motion during exercises. With ResiFit, athletes can seamlessly integrate strength training into skill practice, optimizing their workouts and improving overall performance without the need for expensive equipment.
